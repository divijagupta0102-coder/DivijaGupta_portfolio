Who I Am

A little about
me & my world

Hi! I'm Divija — a 15-year-old with a sketchbook always in hand and a race track under my feet. I study at P.G Vora High School, Mira Road, where I'm known for being the fastest runner, the sharpest dresser, and the girl who can bring any portrait to life with just a pencil.

My favourite colours are black & white — classic, timeless, bold. Just like my art. I believe in working hard, looking good, and chasing every dream at full sprint.
